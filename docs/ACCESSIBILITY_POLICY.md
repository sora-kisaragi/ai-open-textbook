# Accessibility Policy

## Requirements

- Add alt text for meaningful images.
- Do not rely on color alone.
- Use plain structure and descriptive headings.
- Avoid low-contrast diagrams.
- Keep examples inclusive and broadly understandable.
- Provide text alternatives for interactive material when practical.

## Review fields

A review may add:

- `accessibility_status`
- `accessibility_notes`
- `alt_text`
- `needs_accessibility_review`

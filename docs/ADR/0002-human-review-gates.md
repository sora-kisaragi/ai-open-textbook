# ADR 0002: Require Human Review Gates

## Status

Accepted

## Context

AI output can be plausible but wrong.
Educational material affects learners and teachers.

## Decision

Human approval is required before publication.
Machine validation and AI review are necessary but insufficient.

## Consequences

Positive:

- Better accountability.
- Lower risk of publishing wrong answers.
- Clear separation between draft and release.

Negative:

- Slower releases.
- Requires reviewer availability.
